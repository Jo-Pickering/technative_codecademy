Our Mission

Situated in the heart of Stardew Valley, Nerdland Farm prides itself on producing a variety of high quality, organic animal and agricultural products at reasonable prices.

Our Products

Dairy

A selection of eggs, milk, mayonnaise and cheese from our free range chickens, ducks cows, goats and dinosaurs.

Fruit and Veg

Everything from your basics, like cauliflower and green beans, to exotic ancient fruits and start fruit! All grown organically on our Stardew Valley or Ginger Island farms.

Mead, Beer and Wine

If we can grow it, we can make it alcoholic! Fine wines, IPAs and even fruity meads. Not for on the school bus though...

Preserves and Oils

Want to bathe in truffle oil? We've got you covered. Why not enjoy some delicious pickled green beans while you're at it!
